Dr. Sophie Rose Lewis specialises in forest governance and social forestry across Southeast Asia. She collaborates with NGOs, governments and forest communities to conduct applied research, develop capacity, engage in policy advocacy, and provide strategic advisory services aimed at advancing land tenure security and resource rights.

Postdoc, Political Science Chulalongkorn University
PhD, Political Ecology University of British Columbia
MSc, Forest Conservation Imperial College London
