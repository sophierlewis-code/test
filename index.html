About

"A successful consultant must be adaptable and responsive, tailoring outputs to the cultural and environmental contexts shaping forest governance while being proactive in addressing evolving challenges."

With over 15 years of experience working across Southeast Asia, I bring extensive knowledge of forest governance, land and resource rights, and tenure security from the community to the international level.

I specialise in applied research and consulting for non-governmental organisations, collaborating with NGOs, governments, civil society, Indigenous Peoples, and local communities across the region.

I possess strong technical and strategic expertise with extensive experience producing high-quality proposals, technical reports, research papers, policy briefs, and communications outputs.
